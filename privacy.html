Nestly

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Nestly collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Nestly mobile application and related services.

Effective date: March 30, 2026

1. Who We Are

Nestly is a relationship-focused mobile application designed to help couples stay connected through daily rituals, shared reflections, mood check-ins, memories, and lightweight interactive experiences.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored in hashed form, not plain text)
  • Partner username used for pairing
  • Profile and onboarding preferences
  • Daily card responses
  • Mood check-ins and notes
  • Memories, notes, and saved entries
  • Weekly ritual updates
  • Game answers and shared activity data
  • Messages or information you send to us for support

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical information such as app version and device-related diagnostics
  • Usage information necessary to keep the service working, such as authentication status and activity timestamps
  • Security-related logs used to detect abuse, suspicious behavior, or unauthorized access attempts

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate you securely
  • Enable partner pairing and shared couple features
  • Store and display your daily rituals, memories, moods, and activity history
  • Provide in-app experiences such as games, prompts, reflections, and weekly rituals
  • Improve the reliability, safety, and performance of Nestly
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Respond to support requests and legal obligations

4. Partner Visibility

Nestly is built around shared experiences between paired users. If you connect with a partner inside the app, certain content may be visible to that partner as part of the product experience, including:

  • Daily card completion status and responses
  • Mood check-ins
  • Shared game progress and answers where applicable
  • Memories and notes intentionally saved inside your shared space
  • Weekly ritual participation

Please only add content you are comfortable sharing within your paired relationship space.

5. Legal Basis and Purpose

We process your information to provide the Nestly service you request, maintain account security, improve app functionality, and operate core product features.

6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including secure authentication practices, password hashing, access controls, and server-side protections. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

You are responsible for keeping your credentials confidential and for using a strong password.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information unless we are required to retain certain data for legal, security, or operational reasons.

8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following cases:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app and infrastructure
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, and integrity of Nestly, our users, or others
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets

9. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. By using Nestly, you understand that data protection laws may differ depending on where processing occurs.

10. Children’s Privacy

Nestly is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact us at nestlyapp@outlook.com.

12. Account and Subscription Information

If Nestly offers paid plans or subscriptions, payment processing may be handled by Apple or other platform providers. We do not receive or store full payment card details.

13. Third-Party Services

Nestly may rely on third-party tools or infrastructure providers for hosting, analytics, authentication-related operations, or platform services. Those providers may process limited information on our behalf as needed to operate the service.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice inside the app or through other reasonable means.
